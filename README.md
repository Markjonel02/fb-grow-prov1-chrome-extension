🔌 How the Facebook Page Connection Works
The extension uses smart tab detection:

The header shows your connection status in real time:

🟠 "Open Facebook →" — no FB tab open yet
🔵 "Facebook Open" — FB is open but not your Page
🟢 "📄 YourPageName" — your Page is detected and ready


When you click "Send to Your Page":

If your Page tab is already open → content is injected directly into your Page's post composer
If not → it opens Facebook for you, then injects
A floating helper panel appears on the page confirming injection, with a Copy button and step-by-step checklist


After injection, a green badge appears on-page:

"✅ Content ready! Review and click Post →"


📋 Step-by-Step to Use

Install the extension (unzip → chrome://extensions → Load unpacked → fbgrow-v3)
Open your Facebook Page in a tab (e.g. facebook.com/YourPage)
Click the extension icon — the header should go green with your page name
Go to Compose tab → enter a topic → click Generate with AI
Click Send to Your Page → content appears in your Page's composer
Add media if needed → click Post ✅
